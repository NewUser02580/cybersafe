How it works

Link checks combine simple heuristics (HTTP, IP address, suspicious words, private loopback detection) and Google Safe Browsing for public threats (if configured).

Password check

We compute SHA1(password) locally on the server, send only the first 5 characters to HaveIBeenPwned, and compare suffixes. This protects your password privacy.

Quick tips

  • Never reuse passwords across sites.
  • Prefer passphrases (long + memorable) or a password manager.
  • Be cautious of links with IPs, odd ports, or words like 'verify' or 'secure' in unexpected places.
